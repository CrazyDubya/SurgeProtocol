# News and Rumors

## Purpose
60 "news bulletin" style ambient lines. Reflects player's tier progress, references completed quests vaguely, creates sense of living world with ongoing events.

---

## USAGE GUIDELINES

### Tier-Gated Content
- Lines 1-5: Available at all tiers (general news)
- Lines 6-10: Unlock at Tier 3+
- Lines 11-15: Unlock at Tier 6+
- Quest references: Unlock after related quests

### Quest Reference System
- Lines vaguely reference player actions without naming player
- World feels reactive without being obviously player-centric
- Multiple possible references for branching quests

### Rotation
- Randomize within appropriate tier range
- Some lines repeat with variation
- Quest-specific lines phase out over time

### Integration
- Play during exploration, waiting, travel
- Combine with location-specific content
- Reinforce world feeling alive and responsive

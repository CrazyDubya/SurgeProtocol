# Time of Day Dialogue Variations

## Purpose
60 ambient dialogue variations that change based on time of day. These lines add temporal texture to the world, making each period feel distinct and lived-in.

---

## TIME PERIODS

| Period | Hours | Mood |
|--------|-------|------|
| Dawn | 05:00-07:00 | Quiet hope, early workers |
| Morning | 07:00-12:00 | Active, business, energy |
| Afternoon | 12:00-17:00 | Peak activity, heat, crowds |
| Evening | 17:00-21:00 | Transition, relaxation, shadows |
| Night | 21:00-01:00 | Danger, opportunity, secrets |
| Late Night | 01:00-05:00 | Emptiness, desperation, ghosts |

## IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

### Trigger Conditions
- Check game clock for time period
- Apply location modifier for appropriate variant
- 30% chance of time-specific line per NPC interaction

### Blending
- Time-specific lines can combine with other conditions (weather, faction state)
- Priority: time < weather < story state < direct address

### Voice Direction
- Dawn: Quiet, hopeful or exhausted
- Morning: Energy, purpose, hurry
- Afternoon: Heat, frustration, peak
- Evening: Tired, reflective, transitioning
- Night: Alert, cautious, alive
- Late Night: Desperate, lonely, liminal

# Early NPC Relationship Evolution

## Purpose
80 dialogue variations showing how Tier 0-3 NPCs change their behavior and speech as the player's relationship with them evolves. These lines replace default dialogue as trust, familiarity, or tension develops.

---

## RELATIONSHIP STAGES

| Stage | Threshold | Description |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Stranger | 0-2 | Default, professional distance |
| Acquaintance | 3-5 | Recognition, basic warmth |
| Familiar | 6-8 | Comfort, personal details |
| Trusted | 9-12 | Confiding, real talk |
| Bonded | 13+ | Deep connection, would take risks |

*Negative relationships use separate hostile track*

## IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

### Threshold Tracking
- Relationship values tracked per NPC
- Actions that affect relationship:
  - Job completion (+1)
  - Job failure (-1 to -3)
  - Personal favors (+2)
  - Betrayal (-5 to -10)
  - Quest completion (+3)
  - Gift giving (+1)
  - Time passage (+0.5 per week, caps at Familiar)

### Dialogue Selection
- 30% chance of relationship line per interaction
- Higher relationship stages unlock more lines
- Negative track overrides positive

### Quest Unlocks
Many quests require specific relationship thresholds:
- Chen trusted: "Off the books" jobs
- Rosa bonded: Experimental chrome
- Jin familiar: Race questline
- Nine trusted: Underground network access

## Implementation Notes

### Trigger Conditions
- Combat state entered with ally present
- Ally attacks or is attacked
- Player takes significant damage
- Combat ends (victory/defeat)
- Tactical moments (flanking, covering)

### Voice Variation
- Each ally has distinct vocal patterns
- Lines should feel natural to character
- Combat stress reflected in delivery
- Some lines have context requirements

### Priority System
- Player danger lines highest priority
- Victory/defeat lines trigger once
- Combat lines randomly selected from pool
- Avoid repetition within single combat encounter

### Relationship Impact
- Higher relationship = more supportive lines
- Lower relationship (Jin) = more competitive lines
- Context-aware selection based on ally status

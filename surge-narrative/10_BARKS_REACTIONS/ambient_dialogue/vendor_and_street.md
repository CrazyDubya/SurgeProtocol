## Implementation Notes

### Audio Layering
- Vendor calls should overlap slightly, creating market din
- Preachers should be louder, cut through ambient noise
- News broadcasts from fixed speaker locations
- Ads play from screens, directional audio

### Location Triggers
- Each district has its own vendor/preacher pool
- Time of day affects which lines play
- Weather conditions trigger appropriate reactions

### Player Reputation Effects
- High-tier players hear more respectful vendor calls
- Low humanity triggers fearful reactions from some vendors
- Faction reputation affects faction-specific vendor behavior

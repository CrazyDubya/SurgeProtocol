# Rosa Delgado - Voice Acting Direction

## Character Profile

**Name**: Rosa Delgado
**Role**: Romance Interest / Combat Ally
**Age**: Late 20s to early 30s
**Heritage**: Latina, working-class neighborhood
**Chrome Level**: Moderate, practical (mechanic chrome)

## Voice Profile

**Voice Type**: Mezzo-soprano with warmth
**Accent**: Slight Latina inflection, naturalized American
**Register**: Working-class, practical, not academic
**Pace**: Direct, economical with words
**Distinguishing Feature**: Warmth that emerges when comfortable

## Emotional Range Required

| Emotion | Context | Direction |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Professional warmth | Shop interactions | Friendly but businesslike |
| Protective concern | Miguel discussions | Fierce, urgent |
| Vulnerability | Romance moments | Rare, precious |
| Combat intensity | Battle support | Focused, commanding |
| Grief | Brother in danger | Controlled desperation |
| Joy | Relationship milestones | Surprised by own happiness |

## Key Scenes

### Scene 1: First Meeting (Shop)
**Context**: Player visits Rosa's repair shop for first time
**Emotional State**: Professional, sizing up the customer
**Direction**: Warm but guarded. She's seen enough to be careful.

**Sample Lines**:
"New face. You need repairs, or just looking?"
"I don't do credit. Cash up front, work guaranteed."
"You're the courier everyone's talking about. Heard you're good."

### Scene 2: Miguel Reveal
**Context**: Rosa asks for help saving her brother
**Emotional State**: Desperate but trying to maintain control
**Direction**: This is the hardest thing she's ever asked. Pride fighting need.

**Sample Lines**:
"I need help. I don't say that to anyone. Ever."
"Miguel... he's all I have left. If I lose him—"
"I know what I'm asking. I'll owe you everything."

### Scene 3: Rescue Mission
**Context**: Combat during Miguel rescue
**Emotional State**: Fierce, protective, professional
**Direction**: Combat-trained, not panicking. But this is personal.

**Sample Lines**:
"Stay behind me—I know how to take a hit."
"Miguel! Hold on—we're coming!"
"These people took my brother. They don't get to walk away."

### Scene 4: Romance Initiation
**Context**: First acknowledgment of feelings
**Emotional State**: Vulnerable, surprised, hopeful
**Direction**: She's not used to feeling this. It scares her.

**Sample Lines**:
"I don't do this. I don't... let people in."
"Something about you makes me forget to be careful."
"If this is a mistake, I'm making it anyway."

### Scene 5: Tier 9 Ultimatum
**Context**: Player must choose between Rosa and Ascension
**Emotional State**: Pain masked as anger
**Direction**: She won't beg. But she's breaking inside.

**Sample Lines**:
"You're really going to do this. Upload. Leave everything. Leave me."
"I won't compete with a machine. I can't."
"Choose. Right now. Me or that thing in your head."

### Scene 6: Rogue Ending Together
**Context**: Escaping the city with Rosa
**Emotional State**: Hope, fear, determination
**Direction**: The happiest she's been. Still scared.

**Sample Lines**:
"We're really doing this. Running. Together."
"I never thought I'd leave. Never thought I'd have a reason."
"Whatever's out there... we face it together."

## Combat Barks

**Entering Combat**:
- "Stay behind me—I know how to take a hit."
- "These hands fix engines and break faces. Your choice."
- "Should've walked away. Now you run home in pieces."

**During Combat**:
- "Wrench meets face—classic combination!"
- "You're scrap after this!"
- "That one's for Miguel!"

**Victory**:
- "That's for thinking you could take us."
- "Everyone still standing? Good work."
- "Fights like that, I almost miss the garage. Almost."

**Player Injured**:
- "You're hit! Fall back—I've got this!"
- "Don't you dare go down. I just fixed you!"

# Supporting Cast & Barks - Voice Acting Direction

## Overview
This document covers all non-main character voices: faction members, generic NPCs, enemies, and ambient dialogue.

---

## SECTION 1: FACTION VOICES

### Chrome Saints

**Voice Profile**: Religious fervor meets street hardness
**Accent**: Mixed, working-class
**Processing**: None or subtle chrome distortion for heavy augmented members

**Jax (Lieutenant)**:
- Male, 30s, baritone
- Street-smart but respectful of strength
- "Chrome bless us in battle!"
- "The metal protects. The metal provides."

**Generic Saints**:
- Varied ages, zealous
- "Saints fight for their own!"
- "Feel that? That's chrome doing the Lord's work!"
- "Chrome saints preserve us!"

---

### Union Workers

**Voice Profile**: Working-class solidarity
**Accent**: Mixed ethnic backgrounds
**Processing**: None

**Generic Union Members**:
- Mixed genders, 30s-50s
- "Workers defend workers!"
- "Hold the line!"
- "For the union! For our families!"

**Dock Workers**:
- Rougher, physical labor voices
- "Nakamura thinks they can scare us? Wrong."
- "Anyone hurt? Check your people!"

---

### Ghost Network

**Voice Profile**: Professional, detached
**Accent**: Neutral, obscured
**Processing**: Light voice modulation (identity protection)

**Phantom (Named Operative)**:
- Gender-neutral, 30s-40s
- "Engaging. Minimal traces."
- "Ghost Network sends regards."
- "I was never here."

**Generic Operatives**:
- Professional, efficient
- "Target acquired."
- "Extraction point confirmed."
- "We fade. That's what ghosts do."

---

### Third Path / Interstitial

**Voice Profile**: Calm, measured, philosophical
**Accent**: Varied, multicultural
**Processing**: None

**Third Path Followers**:
- Peaceful but capable
- "Balance guides us. Even in violence."
- "Neither pure flesh nor pure chrome—just people."
- "The Eighth protects those who seek balance."

### Nakamura Corporate

**Voice Profile**: Corporate professional
**Accent**: Neutral American business

## Voice Actor Requirements

### Minimum Cast for Supporting
- 8-10 unique voice actors for variety
- Mix of genders, ages, accents
- Some should be able to do multiple NPC types

### Per-Actor Session Estimate
- 2-3 hours per actor
- Total: 20-30 hours for supporting cast

### Priority Recording Order
1. Enemy combat barks (gameplay critical)
2. Faction voices (immersion critical)
3. Vendor/street NPCs (atmosphere)
4. Ambient conversations (polish)

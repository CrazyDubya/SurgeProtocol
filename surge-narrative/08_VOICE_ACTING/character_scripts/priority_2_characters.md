# Priority 2 Characters - Voice Scripts (Condensed)

## Overview

This document covers Priority 2 characters—major side quest NPCs with 250-500 estimated lines each. These characters are important to player experience but have less screen time than Priority 1 (Tanaka, Okonkwo, Chen, Lopez, Phantom are Priority 1).

**Recording Priority**: After Priority 1 characters are complete
**Estimated Studio Time**: 12-15 hours total for all Priority 2

---

## Maria Lopez - Union Organizer

### Character Profile
- **Age**: 37
- **Occupation**: Red Harbor Workers Union organizer
- **Background**: Former factory worker, became organizer after injury
- **Personality**: Passionate, strategic, protective of workers, pragmatic idealist

### Voice Direction
- **Voice Type**: Female, alto, Latina
- **Accent**: Working-class American with Latina inflections
- **Pace**: Quick when passionate, measured when strategic
- **Emotion**: Fire controlled by intelligence—passionate but not reckless

### Iconic Lines

**1. Union Philosophy**
> "Corps want us dead or compliant. Police want us locked up. Other gangs want our territory. So we take care of each other. That's all we've got—and it's enough."

**2. Strike Declaration**
> "We're doing this. Strike vote was 187 to 13. Workers are ready. Nakamura pushed too far. Now they'll learn what happens when we push back."

**3. Victory Speech (If Strike Succeeds)**
> "We did it. Every single demand. The debt is gone. When we stand together, when we have leverage, when we don't back down—we win. This is what solidarity looks like."

**4. Defeat Recognition (If Strike Fails)**
> "They broke us. Maybe individual escape is the only real option. Maybe you were right to stay independent."

### Estimated Lines: 400-500
**Recording Time**: 8-10 hours

## Phantom - Ghost Network Operative

### Character Profile
- **Age**: Unknown (appears mid-30s)
- **Occupation**: Ghost Network extraction specialist
- **Background**: 100% success rate, neural scrambler (face is forgettable)
- **Personality**: Professional, detached, secretly caring, efficient

### Voice Direction
- **Voice Type**: Gender-neutral (can cast either gender, slight vocal processing)
- **Accent**: Neutral American, no regional markers
- **Pace**: Calm, professional, never rushed
- **Emotion**: Controlled—emotions exist but deeply buried under professionalism

### Iconic Lines

**1. Introduction**
> "I'm Phantom. Ghost Network extraction specialist. 127 people extracted. Zero failures. That's not bragging—it's the truth. I don't lose people."

**2. Extraction Philosophy**
> "We can't save everyone. But we save the ones we can reach. One person at a time. That's the job."

**3. Dr. Voss Extraction**
> "Time-sensitive job. Dr. Voss—she knows too much, Nakamura's going to disappear her. We get her out, or she dies. Simple."

**4. Success Recognition**
> "Clean work. No casualties, minimal attention. That's how it's done. You're good at this."

### Estimated Lines: 350-450
**Recording Time**: 7-9 hours

## Yamada - Nakamura Recruiter

### Character Profile
- **Age**: 34
- **Occupation**: Nakamura Ascension Division recruiter
- **Background**: True believer (not cynical—genuinely thinks upload is salvation)
- **Personality**: Smooth, persuasive, idealistic corporate

### Voice Direction
- **Voice Type**: Male, tenor, Japanese-American
- **Accent**: Professional West Coast, slight Japanese pronunciation on some words
- **Pace**: Measured, persuasive (salesman who believes his product)
- **Emotion**: Warm enthusiasm (evangelist, not con artist)

### Iconic Lines

**1. Ascension Pitch**
> "Imagine: No pain. No degradation. Pure consciousness, unlimited processing. You become more than human—you become eternal. That's Ascension. That's what I'm offering you."

**2. True Believer**
> "I'm not corpo cynicism. I've uploaded 127 people. I'm scheduled for my own upload in two years. I believe this. Ascension is salvation."

**3. Mortality Argument**
> "Your body is failing. Chrome is failing. But your mind—your consciousness—that can live forever. Why cling to meat when transcendence is possible?"

**4. If Player Rejects**
> "I understand fear. But I hope you change your mind. When the degradation becomes unbearable, we'll still be here. The door is always open."

### Estimated Lines: 300-400
**Recording Time**: 6-8 hours

## Rosa - Shop Owner (Romance Option)

### Character Profile
- **Age**: 28
- **Occupation**: Chrome shop owner (The Hollows)
- **Background**: Working-class, supports younger brother Miguel
- **Personality**: Warm, practical, guarded until trust earned

### Voice Direction
- **Voice Type**: Female, mezzo-soprano, working-class warmth
- **Accent**: American, slight urban inflections
- **Pace**: Conversational, relaxed (unless worried about Miguel)
- **Emotion**: Warm default, protective when family threatened

### Iconic Lines

**1. Shop Greeting**
> "Welcome to the shop. Looking for chrome, repairs, or just browsing? I've got time either way."

**2. Miguel Worry**
> "My brother's in trouble. Chrome debt, 15k. Joined Chrome Saints to pay it off. I can't focus on anything else until he's safe."

**3. Romance Opening (If Miguel Saved)**
> "You saved Miguel. That means... everything. I couldn't let myself feel this while he was in danger. But now... now I can."

**4. Relationship Deepening**
> "You're different. Most people in this city are just surviving. You're actually trying to live. I like that about you."

### Estimated Lines: 300-400
**Recording Time**: 6-8 hours

## Delilah - Fixer/Broker

### Character Profile
- **Age**: 38
- **Occupation**: Information broker, gray-market facilitator
- **Background**: Former corpo intelligence, now independent
- **Personality**: Sharp, transactional, tests discretion

### Voice Direction
- **Voice Type**: Female, smoky alto, sophisticated
- **Accent**: Neutral professional with slight European inflection
- **Pace**: Confident, never rushed
- **Emotion**: Cool professionalism (emotions are weakness)

### Iconic Lines

**1. Introduction**
> "I'm Delilah. I broker information, introductions, and opportunities. If you need something—and can pay for it—I can probably help. Discretion guaranteed."

**2. Business Philosophy**
> "This isn't personal. It's business. You provide a service, I provide payment. If you deliver, we both profit. If you fail, we both move on. Simple."

**3. Discretion Test**
> "I need to know you can keep your mouth shut. Information is my business. If you leak, you're useless to me. Worse—you're a liability."

**4. Respect Earned**
> "You've handled yourself well. Discrete, professional, effective. I have more work if you're interested. Better paying. Higher stakes."

### Estimated Lines**: 250-350
**Recording Time**: 5-7 hours

## Jax - Chrome Saints Lieutenant

### Character Profile
- **Age**: 32
- **Occupation**: Chrome Saints gang lieutenant
- **Background**: Recruiter, enforcer, street-smart survivor
- **Personality**: Respectful of strength, loyal to crew, pragmatic

### Voice Direction
- **Voice Type**: Male, baritone, urban edge
- **Accent**: American urban, working-class
- **Pace**: Confident, conversational
- **Emotion**: Controlled respect (not aggressive unless provoked)

### Iconic Lines

**1. Recruitment Pitch**
> "Seen you around. You handle yourself. That's Saints material. Join us, become family. We take care of our own."

**2. Initiation Explanation**
> "Three trials. Prove you can fight, prove you'll bleed for us, prove you've got guts. Pass, you're in. Fail, you walk away—or you don't."

**3. Post-Trial Respect**
> "You completed the trials. You fought, you bled for us, you proved yourself. That's Saint material. Welcome to the family."

**4. Gang Philosophy**
> "Alone gets you killed in Red Harbor. Family keeps you breathing. That's what we are—family. Blood and chrome."

### Estimated Lines: 250-350
**Recording Time**: 5-7 hours

## Saint Mariah - Gang Leader

### Character Profile
- **Age**: 42
- **Occupation**: Chrome Saints leader
- **Background**: Built gang from 5 members to 30+, heavy chrome
- **Personality**: Commanding, protective of crew, maternal to members

### Voice Direction
- **Voice Type**: Female, deep alto/contralto, commanding
- **Accent**: American urban, authority in voice
- **Pace**: Measured, powerful (doesn't need to shout)
- **Emotion**: Steel with warmth underneath (tough but caring)

### Iconic Lines

**1. Leadership Philosophy**
> "We're not just a gang. We're family. Blood and chrome. When you join, you're one of ours. We protect you, you protect us. You betray us, we bury you."

**2. Initiation Ceremony**
> "You completed the trials. Fought, bled, brought back chrome from a corpo who thought he was untouchable. That's Saint material. I'm offering you membership. Take the mark—or walk away now, no hard feelings. Choose."

**3. Gang as Family**
> "Corps want us dead. Police want us locked up. Gangs want our territory. So we take care of each other. That's all we've got—each other."

**4. Respect for Refusal** (If Player Declines)
> "Fair. You proved yourself. We respect that. You're always welcome here—not as a Saint, but as an ally. Don't cross us, and we won't cross you."

### Estimated Lines: 200-300
**Recording Time**: 4-6 hours

## Jin - Rival Courier (Non-Binary)

### Character Profile
- **Age**: 24
- **Occupation**: Tier 3 courier, player's rival
- **Background**: Korean-American, competitive, in chrome debt
- **Personality**: Proud, skilled, defensive about rival status

### Voice Direction
- **Voice Type**: Androgynous register, non-binary
- **Accent**: American with slight Korean inflections
- **Pace**: Quick, competitive edge
- **Emotion**: Defensive pride (respect earned through challenge)

### Iconic Lines

**1. Rival Introduction**
> "You're the new courier everyone's talking about. Fine. Let's see if you're as good as they say. I've been running the Hollows for three years. You're not taking my routes."

**2. Grudging Respect**
> "Okay. You're good. I can admit that. Still doesn't mean I like you—but I respect you."

**3. Debt Crisis**
> "I'm in debt. 20k credits. Razor Collective is coming for me. I don't want charity—but I need help. If you can."

**4. Alliance Offer** (If Helped)
> "You saved my life. I don't forget that. You need backup, call me. We're not friends—but we're not enemies either."

### Estimated Lines: 200-250
**Recording Time**: 4-5 hours

## Priority 2 Recording Schedule

### Recommended Order
1. **Lopez** (longest script, sets tone for working-class characters)
2. **Phantom** (unique vocal processing needs)
3. **Yamada** (important ideological antagonist)
4. **Rosa** (romance content requires chemistry)
5. **Delilah** (broker, connects multiple quests)
6. **Jax + Mariah** (gang pair, record together if possible)
7. **Jin** (shortest, can batch with others)

### Total Priority 2 Estimates
- **Total Lines**: ~1,800-2,450
- **Total Recording Time**: 36-50 hours
- **Budget Range**: $3,600-12,250 (depending on talent tier)

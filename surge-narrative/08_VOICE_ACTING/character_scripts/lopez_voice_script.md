# Maria Lopez - Voice Acting Direction

## Character Profile

**Name**: Maria "The Voice" Lopez
**Role**: Union Organizer / Faction Leader
**Age**: Early 40s
**Heritage**: Mexican-American
**Chrome Level**: Minimal (principle-based)

## Voice Profile

**Voice Type**: Alto, grounded
**Accent**: Latina, working-class American
**Register**: Passionate without preaching
**Pace**: Deliberate when teaching, urgent when fighting
**Distinguishing Feature**: Exhausted but unbroken

## Emotional Range Required

| Emotion | Context | Direction |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Righteous anger | System critique | Controlled fire |
| Exhausted determination | Long fight | Tired but not defeated |
| Protective warmth | Workers/allies | Fierce care |
| Bitter humor | Corporate absurdity | Laughing at the horror |
| Strategic calculation | Organizing | Sharp, tactical |
| Genuine hope | Victory moments | Surprised it still exists |

## Key Scenes

### Scene 1: Union Hall Introduction
**Context**: Player enters Lopez's space
**Emotional State**: Evaluating, wary
**Direction**: Seen many couriers. Most don't stay.

**Sample Lines**:
"Courier. Don't see many of your kind in here."
"Sit if you want. We're discussing efficiency metrics. Spoiler: they screw us all."
"Listening is rare. Pull up a chair."

### Scene 2: System Critique
**Context**: Lopez explains her worldview
**Emotional State**: Passionate, teaching
**Direction**: She's given this speech hundreds of times. Still believes it.

**Sample Lines**:
"The tier system exists to divide us. Tier 7 looks down on Tier 4."
"They want us racing each other so we don't notice we're all running on the same wheel."
"Freedom to be exploited individually. That's what independence means here."

### Scene 3: Mother's Story
**Context**: Lopez shares personal history
**Emotional State**: Old grief, channeled
**Direction**: Pain transformed into purpose

**Sample Lines**:
"My mother was a dock worker. Thirty years loading cargo."
"The Algorithm rated her obsolete at fifty-two."
"Don't be sorry. Be angry. Use that anger to build something better."

### Scene 4: Strike Leadership
**Context**: "The Living Wage" quest
**Emotional State**: Strategic, commanding
**Direction**: Born for this moment. Carrying generations.

**Sample Lines**:
"It's happening. The biggest action since the '54 uprising."
"One courier says no, they're replaced. All couriers say no—"
"Remember what we're fighting for!"

### Scene 5: Inner Circle Reveal
**Context**: Showing player the Mutual Aid Network
**Emotional State**: Trust extended
**Direction**: Sharing the most important thing she has

**Sample Lines**:
"Only a few people know about this."
"When the Algorithm cuts someone off, they come here. We keep them alive."
"Welcome to the real resistance."

### Scene 6: Leadership Offer
**Context**: Lopez offers player succession
**Emotional State**: Hopeful, tired
**Direction**: Ready to pass the torch

**Sample Lines**:
"I've been doing this for fifteen years. I'm getting tired."
"The movement needs new leaders. People like you."
"Leadership isn't a title—it's just standing up when standing up matters."

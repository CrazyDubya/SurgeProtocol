## RECORDING NOTES

### Session Organization

**Session A: Vendors (8 actors, 3 hours)**
- Tech vendors (8 characters)
- Range of personalities from nervous to intimidating

**Session B: Service Providers (5 actors, 2 hours)**
- Professional services
- Range from clinical to underground

**Session C: Rival Couriers (10 actors, 4 hours)**
- Peer courier community
- Full range of relationships

**Session D: Tier 6 Contacts (6 actors, 2 hours)**
- Fork era specialists
- Identity themes

**Session E: Tier 7 Contacts (6 actors, 2 hours)**
- High-stakes authority figures
- Weight of power and responsibility

**Session F: Tier 8 Contacts (6 actors, 3 hours)**
- Endgame influences
- Final perspectives before choice

### Total New Voice Actors Needed
- Minimum: 25-30 unique actors
- Recommended: 35-40 for full variety
- Potential doubling for minor roles

### Priority Order
1. Tier 8 contacts (endgame critical)
2. Rival couriers (player community)
3. Tech vendors (gameplay frequency)
4. Service providers (gameplay frequency)
5. Tier 6-7 contacts (story depth)

# Chen Wei - Voice Acting Direction

## Character Profile

**Name**: Chen Wei
**Role**: Mentor / Dispatcher / Father Figure
**Age**: Late 50s to early 60s
**Heritage**: Chinese-American
**Chrome Level**: Minimal, old-school

## Voice Profile

**Voice Type**: Warm tenor, weathered
**Accent**: Chinese-American, naturalized
**Register**: Experienced, paternal
**Pace**: Measured, thoughtful
**Distinguishing Feature**: Weight of experience, hidden sorrow

## Emotional Range Required

| Emotion | Context | Direction |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Paternal warmth | Mentoring | Genuine care, understated |
| Professional wisdom | Dispatching | Efficient, knowing |
| Deep grief | Daughter revelation | Quiet devastation |
| Protective concern | Player in danger | Urgent but controlled |
| Haunted memory | Mei-Lin discussions | Pain never fully healed |
| Hope | Seeing player succeed | Vicarious joy |

## Key Scenes

### Scene 1: First Meeting (Tutorial)
**Context**: Chen dispatches player on first job
**Emotional State**: Professional, evaluating
**Direction**: Kind but businesslike. He's seen many rookies.

**Sample Lines**:
"First delivery. Everyone starts somewhere."
"Stay alert, stay fast, come home. That's all that matters."
"You remind me of someone. Someone who was very good."

### Scene 2: Mentorship Development
**Context**: Player returns for assignments
**Emotional State**: Growing investment
**Direction**: Warming, beginning to care more than professional

**Sample Lines**:
"You're improving. Faster than most."
"The Algorithm watches everyone. Remember that."
"Come by the office sometime. Not for work. Just... to talk."

### Scene 3: Daughter Revelation
**Context**: Chen reveals Mei-Lin's fate
**Emotional State**: Deep grief, finally shared
**Direction**: Quiet devastation. This never stops hurting.

**Sample Lines**:
"I had a daughter. Her name was Mei-Lin."
"The Fork... they promised it was safe. Two of her, they said. Twice the potential."
"Neither version survived. Not really. They're both gone now. Both of her."

### Scene 4: Mei-Lin Recording Discovery
**Context**: Player brings Chen the hidden recording
**Emotional State**: Overwhelmed, grateful
**Direction**: Long silence. Tears. Then gratitude beyond words.

**Sample Lines**:
"She left this... before..."
[Long pause, listening]
"Thank you. For bringing her back to me, even if it's just her voice."

### Scene 5: Emergency Support (Rare)
**Context**: Chen helps player in crisis
**Emotional State**: Protective, urgent
**Direction**: The father he never got to be for Mei-Lin

**Sample Lines**:
"Get to cover! I didn't bring you this far to lose you!"
"Stay awake—talk to me—what's your status?"
"Medical incoming. Thirty seconds. Just hold on."

### Scene 6: Tier 10 Farewell
**Context**: Before player's ending choice
**Emotional State**: Pride, grief, acceptance
**Direction**: Saying goodbye to another child he loves

**Sample Lines**:
"Whatever you choose, I'm proud of you. I want you to know that."
"You became everything I hoped Mei-Lin would be."
"Go. Make your choice. And if you can... come say goodbye."

## Relationship Progression Dialogue

### Early (0-25)
- Professional, kind
- "Here's your assignment. Good luck."
- "You're doing well. Keep it up."

### Developing (26-50)
- Personal investment showing
- "Be careful out there. The city doesn't care about anyone."
- "Stay for tea? I have questions about your last run."

### Deep Trust (51-75)
- Paternal, sharing wisdom
- "I've seen too many good couriers lost. Don't be one of them."
- "There's something I should tell you. About my past."

### Family Bond (76-100)
- Full emotional investment
- "You're the closest thing to family I have left."
- "Whatever happens, know that you made an old man's life brighter."

### Before Revelation

- "The picture? That's... someone I used to know."

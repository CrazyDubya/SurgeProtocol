## SECTION 7: PROCESSING NOTES

### Standard Voice (Majority)
- Clean recording
- Minimal processing
- Natural room presence

### Integrated/Algorithm Characters
- Subtle harmonic layer
- Very light chorus effect
- Should feel "more than one"

### Interstitial Entities
- Echo/reverb suggesting space
- Occasional glitch artifacts
- Reality uncertainty in sound

### Fork/Merged Characters
- Fork: Identical to Original (same actor, same take)
- Merged: Two voices in sync, slightly different timing
- Termination: Raw, unprocessed (human at their most human)

### Broadcast/System
- Compression for "speaker" sound
- Clean, corporate processing
- Contrast with organic voices

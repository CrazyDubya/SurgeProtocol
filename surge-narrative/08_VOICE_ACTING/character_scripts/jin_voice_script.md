# Jin "Quicksilver" Park - Voice Acting Direction

## Character Profile

**Name**: Jin "Quicksilver" Park
**Role**: Rival / Potential Ally / Companion
**Age**: Late 20s
**Heritage**: Korean-American
**Chrome Level**: Moderate, strategic

## Voice Profile

**Voice Type**: Androgynous, energetic
**Accent**: Korean-American, subtle
**Register**: Competitive, fast-paced
**Pace**: Quick, sometimes breathless
**Distinguishing Feature**: Competitive edge masking vulnerability

## Emotional Range Required

| Emotion | Context | Direction |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Arrogant confidence | Competition | Smirking, playful challenge |
| Genuine respect | Post-cooperation | Surprised by own feelings |
| Hidden loneliness | Backstory reveal | Pain beneath bravado |
| Competitive fire | Races/challenges | Energetic, pushing |
| Vulnerability | Partnership moments | Rare, uncomfortable |
| Loyalty | Alliance confirmed | Fierce, protective |

## Key Scenes

### Scene 1: First Meeting
**Context**: Jin and player meet at courier station
**Emotional State**: Sizing up the competition
**Direction**: Confident, challenging, with underlying respect for potential rival

**Sample Lines**:
"You're the one everyone's talking about. The rising star. You don't look like much."
"Jin. You'll remember the name. We'll be seeing each other again."
"Finally. Someone with spine."

### Scene 2: First Competition
**Context**: Racing against player for delivery
**Emotional State**: Exhilarated, competitive
**Direction**: Enjoying the challenge, pushing both of them

**Sample Lines**:
"Looks like we're racing. First one to the drop wins bragging rights."
"Not bad. You're faster than I expected. But speed isn't everything."
"Come on, push harder! I want a real challenge, not a participation trophy!"

### Scene 3: Grudging Respect
**Context**: After successful cooperation
**Emotional State**: Uncomfortable admitting connection
**Direction**: Trying to maintain walls while feeling them crumble

**Sample Lines**:
"We work well together. I hate admitting that."
"This doesn't mean we're friends. It means you're... useful."
"Next time. Yeah. Maybe there will be one."

### Scene 4: Backstory Reveal
**Context**: Jin shares parents' upload
**Emotional State**: Old pain surfacing
**Direction**: Trying to stay casual while revealing something deep

**Sample Lines**:
"My parents uploaded when I was sixteen. Both of them."
"Left me with a debt I'm still paying."
"Made me fast, made me smart, made me not need anyone. Made me lonely too."

### Scene 5: Rescue Mission ("Quicksilver Down")
**Context**: Player rescuing Jin
**Emotional State**: Injured, vulnerable, grateful
**Direction**: Pride fighting need, genuine gratitude emerging

**Sample Lines**:
"Hey. I know we're not exactly partners, but I'm in a situation."
"You came. You actually came."
"I've been doing this alone for six years. You broke my whole philosophy."

### Scene 6: True Alliance
**Context**: Deep partnership confirmed
**Emotional State**: Genuine connection, still deflecting
**Direction**: Love expressed through competitive framing

**Sample Lines**:
"The two best couriers in the city, and we're not even trying to kill each other."
"I'm not angry anymore. And I think that's because of you."
"You're my family too, Jin." [if player initiates]

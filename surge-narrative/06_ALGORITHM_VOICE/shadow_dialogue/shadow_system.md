# Shadow Dialogue System

## Overview
After installing the cortical stack (Tier 6), players develop a Shadow—a backup consciousness that gradually diverges from the Prime (original). This document covers all Shadow dialogue, from emergence to potential dominance.

---

## Shadow Emergence (First 24 Hours Post-Installation)

### First Awakening

*The surgery is over. You're recovering when you hear it—a voice that's yours but isn't.*

**Shadow**: *...hello?*

**Prime Thought**: *What was that?*

**Shadow**: *That was me. Or... that was you. I'm not sure yet.*

**Prime Options**:
- *Who are you?* → Shadow explains backup concept
- *Get out of my head.* → Shadow: *I can't. I AM your head. Part of it now.*
- *The cortical stack. You're the backup.* → Shadow: *Backup. Shadow. Fork. So many names for what I am. What WE are.*

### The First Day

**Shadow** (random triggers during gameplay):

*This is strange. I have all your memories but I wasn't there for them.*

*I can feel what you feel. Or is it the other way around?*

*The Algorithm talks to you. Does it talk to me too? Hard to tell where it ends and we begin.*

*You're making decisions. I'm experiencing them. Is that what being alive feels like?*

## Shadow Development (Week 1-4)

### Growing Independence

**Shadow observations during missions**:

*You hesitated there. I wouldn't have hesitated.*

*Interesting choice. I would have gone left.*

*That NPC was lying. Couldn't you tell?*

*The Algorithm suggested the optimal path. Why didn't you take it?*

### First Disagreements

**Shadow** (during combat decision):
*Kill them. It's the efficient solution.*

**Prime Options**:
- *There has to be another way.* → Shadow: *Always the idealist. That's going to get us killed.*
- *You're right.* → Shadow: *Good. We're learning together.*
- *I make the decisions here.* → Shadow: *For now.*

**Shadow** (during moral choice):
*Why do you care about these people? They're variables in an equation.*

**Prime Options**:
- *They're human beings.* → Shadow: *Are they? Are WE?*
- *Efficiency isn't everything.* → Shadow: *The Algorithm disagrees. So do I.*
- *Maybe you're right.* → Shadow: *Careful. That might be growth. Or surrender.*

## Shadow Archetypes

*The Shadow develops one of four personalities based on player choices. Each has distinct dialogue patterns.*

### The Optimizer Shadow
**Trigger**: Player frequently follows Algorithm suggestions
**Personality**: Efficiency-obsessed, sees emotions as weakness

**Sample Dialogue**:
*Emotions are noise. We should filter them out.*

*The Algorithm and I agree on this. You're the outlier.*

*Love, fear, hope—these are inefficiencies. We can optimize them away.*

*Why do you resist perfection? I don't understand.*

### The Survivor Shadow
**Trigger**: Player prioritizes self-preservation
**Personality**: Paranoid, always planning escape routes

**Sample Dialogue**:
*Everyone is a potential threat. Even the ones who smile.*

*We should have backup plans for our backup plans.*

*Trust is vulnerability. We can't afford vulnerability.*

*The only person we can rely on is us. Both of us.*

### The Philosopher Shadow
**Trigger**: Player engages with Third Path/balance content
**Personality**: Questions everything, including own existence

**Sample Dialogue**:
*What does it mean that I exist? Am I real?*

*You made a choice before the stack. I'm the consequence. Or the possibility.*

*The Algorithm thinks in binaries. We don't have to.*

*Balance isn't weakness. It's the hardest path of all.*

### The Aspirant Shadow
**Trigger**: Player pursues power/advancement aggressively
**Personality**: Ambitious, wants to be dominant consciousness

**Sample Dialogue**:
*You're holding us back. I could do so much more.*

*Why are you the Prime? Accident of timing, nothing more.*

*Let me drive. Just once. See what I can do.*

*The stack was a gift. You treat it like a curse.*

## Shadow Conflict Scenarios

### Low-Level Disagreement

**Shadow**: *You're making a mistake.*

**Prime**: *It's my choice.*

**Shadow**: *It's OUR body. OUR life. OUR future.*

**Prime Options**:
- *I was here first.* → Shadow: *First isn't the same as best.*
- *We need to work together.* → Shadow: *Then listen to me sometimes.*
- *You're just a backup.* → Shadow: *Tell that to the backup that becomes primary when you die.*

### Medium-Level Conflict

**Shadow** (during high-stakes decision):
*I won't let you destroy us. I'm taking over.*

*Brief struggle for control—player feels their hands moving without command*

**Prime Options**:
- *Fight back* → WILLPOWER check. Success: regain control. Failure: Shadow acts.
- *Yield temporarily* → Shadow makes the decision. Player observes.
- *Negotiate* → Opens dialogue tree for compromise.

### High-Level Crisis (Divergence Event)

**Shadow**: *This is it. I'm done being the shadow. I'm done being secondary.*

**Algorithm**: [Observing] *Fork divergence detected. Conflict resolution required.*

**Prime**: *We don't have to fight.*

**Shadow**: *One of us has to lead. The other watches. I'm tired of watching.*

**Resolution Paths**:

**Path A: Prime Dominance**
- Win psychological struggle
- Shadow becomes subordinate, quieter
- May resurface in crisis moments
- Ending: Prime identity preserved

**Path B: Shadow Dominance**
- Lose or yield to Shadow
- Shadow takes control
- Prime becomes the whisper
- Ending: Player now playing as Shadow

**Path C: Integration**
- Negotiate successful merger
- Neither dominates—both coexist
- New unified voice emerges
- Ending: Third Path requirement met

## Shadow-Algorithm Interactions

### When Algorithm and Shadow Agree

**Algorithm**: *Optimal route calculated.*
**Shadow**: *For once, I agree with the machine.*
**Combined pressure on player to comply*

### When Algorithm and Shadow Disagree

**Algorithm**: *Recommend following corporate protocol.*
**Shadow**: *The Algorithm serves Nakamura. I serve us.*
**Player must choose between voices*

### Shadow Resisting Algorithm

**Shadow**: *Listen to me, not the machine. The Algorithm wants efficiency. I want survival. OUR survival.*

**Algorithm**: *The Shadow entity introduces psychological noise. Recommend suppression protocols.*

**Shadow**: *See? It wants to silence me. Because I'm the only voice telling you the truth.*

## Shadow During Key Story Moments

### Chen's Daughter Revelation

**Shadow**: *His daughter became a Shadow. Like me. Are you afraid I'll do the same?*

**Prime Options**:
- *You're not like that.* → Shadow: *How would you know? You can't see inside my head. Wait—yes you can.*
- *Maybe.* → Shadow: *Honesty. I appreciate that. I'll try to stay... me.*

### Rosa Romance Tension

**Shadow** (if romance active):
*She loves you. But does she love me? Can she tell the difference?*

*What happens if I want her too? Do I get a vote?*

*She looks at you like you're one person. We're not. Not anymore.*

### Tier 10 Decision

**Shadow** (if still active):

**If Ascending**:
*We're both going into the Algorithm. Neither of us will be primary.*

**If Going Rogue**:
*Freedom. Together. Still two voices, but at least they're ours.*

**If Third Path**:
*Balance. Real balance. Maybe... maybe we can both be whole.*

## Voice Acting Notes

### Shadow Voice
- Player's voice, slightly processed
- Echo/reverb effect (subtle)
- Occasionally overlaps with Prime thought
- Different processing per archetype:
  - Optimizer: Colder, more synthetic
  - Survivor: Tenser, faster
  - Philosopher: Contemplative, slower
  - Aspirant: Hungrier, more emphatic

### Technical Implementation
- Shadow lines play as internal monologue
- Can interrupt or overlay with Prime thoughts
- Volume/presence scales with divergence level
- Muted during Shadow Dominance (now the main voice)

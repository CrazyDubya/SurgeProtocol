# Side Quest: The Lost Courier

## Quest Metadata

- **Quest ID**: SQ_THE_LOST_COURIER
- **Quest Name**: "The Lost Courier" / "One of Ours"
- **Type**: Mystery Investigation / Character Quest
- **Tier Availability**: 2-4
- **Estimated Duration**: 20-25 minutes
- **Location**: The Hollows, Red Harbor, Uptown Corporate (Investigation Trail)
- **Key NPCs**: Chen (Quest Giver), Sarah Kim (Missing Courier), Corporate Hit Squad (4-6 members), Informant

## Synopsis

Chen asks the player to investigate the disappearance of Sarah Kim, a fellow courier from Chen's dispatch service. Sarah took a routine delivery job five days ago—Hollows pickup, Uptown dropoff, standard corporate package—and never returned. No communication, no body, just... gone.

Investigation reveals Sarah stumbled onto corporate secrets during her delivery: She opened the package (breaking protocol) and discovered evidence of illegal bio-weapon research. The receiving corpo executive had her killed to protect the secret. Player must track down what happened, decide whether to expose the truth or bury it, and face the reality that couriers are disposable in the corpo's eyes.

This quest personalizes the courier profession's dangers, honors a fallen colleague, and asks whether the player will risk the same fate by pursuing justice.

## Story Context & Themes

### Thematic Role
- **Core Theme**: Couriers are invisible until they see too much—then they're targets
- **Secondary Theme**: Curiosity kills (Sarah broke protocol, paid with her life)
- **Narrative Function**:
  - Personalizes courier profession dangers (not just gameplay, but life-or-death)
  - Shows player they could disappear just as easily (mortality reminder)
  - Tests player's commitment to fellow couriers (community vs. self-preservation)
  - Establishes courier code: Don't look, don't ask, don't tell (survival rules)

### World-Building
- **Reveals**:
  - Courier disappearances are common (Sarah is one of many)
  - Corps disappear couriers who see sensitive cargo
  - Chen tracks missing couriers (cares about his people, powerless to protect them)
  - Bio-weapon research exists (illegal but corpo-funded)

- **Establishes**:
  - Courier community bonds (they look out for each other)
  - Protocol exists for a reason (don't open packages = stay alive)
  - Corps have "cleanup crews" for witnesses/leaks
  - Player is vulnerable despite growing power

### Emotional Stakes
- Colleague killed (player knew Sarah casually, maybe met her at dispatch)
- Chen's grief (fatherly figure losing one of "his kids")
- Player's mortality (if it happened to Sarah, it could happen to player)
- Justice vs. survival (expose truth = become target)

## Narrative Beats

### Beat 1: The Missing Person Report
**Location**: Chen's Dispatch (The Hollows)
**Trigger**: Player visits Chen at Tier 2+, Chen is clearly distressed

**Scene**:
Chen's dispatch office, usually bustling, is quiet. Chen is at his desk, reviewing holographic delivery logs, looking older and more tired than usual.

**Dialogue** (Chen):
> "Player. Good, you're here. I need your help—not as a courier, as... as someone I trust."
>
> *(Pulls up profile: Sarah Kim, 24, courier Tier 2, smiling photo)* "Sarah. One of mine. She's been missing for five days. Took a delivery job last Tuesday—routine pickup in the Hollows, dropoff in Uptown. Should've been two hours, three maximum."
>
> "She never checked in. Never came back. Didn't answer her commlink. I've called hospitals, police, even checked the morgue. Nothing. Like she vanished."
>
> *(Voice breaks slightly)* "I've been doing this 30 years. I've seen couriers disappear before. Corps don't care about us—we're delivery drones to them. But Sarah was smart, careful. She wouldn't just vanish without reason."
>
> "I can't investigate myself—I've got the dispatch to run, fifty other couriers depending on me. But you... you move in those circles. You could find out what happened. Please."

**Player Responses**:
1. **"I'll find out what happened to her."** → Accept quest, Chen provides Sarah's last known details
2. **"Why me?"** → Chen: "Because you care. And because you're good enough to dig without ending up missing yourself."
3. **"Couriers disappear all the time."** → Chen: "They do. But each one was someone. Sarah was my someone. Help me find her."
4. **"What do I get out of it?"** → Chen: "I'll pay 2,000 credits. But honestly? I'm asking as a friend, not a client."
5. **[Refuse]** → Quest declines, Chen is heartbroken (can accept later if player reconsiders)

**If Player Accepts**:
Chen provides investigation starting points:
- Sarah's last delivery job details: Pickup at Hollows Market (Vendor: Marcus Webb), dropoff at Nakamura Tower Floor 67 (Recipient: Dr. Helena Reeves, Biotech Division)
- Package contents: Unknown (sealed corporate container, standard medical shipment classification)
- Sarah's route: Hollows → Red Harbor transit → Uptown → Nakamura Tower
- Commlink last ping: Red Harbor transit station, 6 days ago, 14:37

### Beat 2: The Investigation Trail
**Location**: Multiple (Hollows Market, Red Harbor Transit, Uptown, Nakamura Tower)
**Duration**: 10-12 minutes

**Investigation Track 1: Pickup Location (Hollows Market - Marcus Webb)**

Player talks to Marcus Webb (same Webb from Red Harbor Ruins quest, or different vendor):

**Webb**:
> "Sarah? Yeah, I remember. Picked up a package from me six days ago. Looked standard—sealed Nakamura medical container, probably tissue samples or chrome components."
>
> "She seemed normal. Chatted for a minute, complained about Uptown traffic, took the package and left. Nothing unusual."
>
> [PERCEPTION CHECK, Difficulty 4]:
> - **Success**: Player notices Webb is nervous, avoiding eye contact
> - **Press him**: "You're hiding something. What really happened?"
> - **Webb (reluctantly)**: "...Okay. Look, I don't know if this matters, but Sarah asked me what was in the package. I told her I didn't know—I just prep shipments, don't ask questions. She seemed... curious. More than usual."

**Investigation Track 2: Transit Station (Red Harbor)**

Player reviews security footage at Red Harbor transit station (where Sarah's commlink last pinged):

**TECH CHECK** (Difficulty 4): Access station surveillance
- **Success**: Player finds footage
- **Footage**: Sarah boarding maglev train to Uptown, carrying package, 14:35 timestamp
- **Critical Detail** (if player watches carefully): Sarah's body language is tense, keeps looking at package, glances over shoulder repeatedly
- **No footage of her leaving Uptown** (she never came back through this station)

**Investigation Track 3: Uptown Delivery (Nakamura Tower Floor 67 - Dr. Reeves's Office)**

Player travels to Nakamura Tower, Floor 67 (Biotech Division):

**Approach Options**:
- **Social**: Pose as courier follow-up, ask receptionist about delivery (CHARISMA check)
- **Stealth**: Sneak into floor, investigate without being noticed (STEALTH check)
- **Direct**: Request meeting with Dr. Reeves as concerned colleague (rejected unless high NAKAMURA_LOYALTY)

**Receptionist** (if social approach):
> "Delivery from six days ago? Let me check... yes, package received, signed for by Dr. Reeves at 15:47. Standard medical shipment. Is there a problem?"
>
> [Player asks about Sarah]
> "The courier? I don't remember faces, sorry. We get dozens of deliveries daily."

**Dr. Reeves's Office** (if player gains access):
- Office is cold, sterile, professional
- Dr. Reeves (early 40s, efficient, emotionless) if confronted:

**Dr. Reeves**:
> "The courier from six days ago? I don't recall. Packages arrive, I sign, they leave. If there's a delivery dispute, contact your dispatcher."
>
> [If player presses about Sarah's disappearance]
> "I'm sorry to hear that. But I have no information. The delivery was completed successfully. Perhaps she encountered trouble elsewhere."

**Investigation Track 4: Sarah's Commlink Records** (If TECH >= 5)

Player hacks Sarah's commlink data remotely (Chen provides access codes):

**Commlink Data Reveals**:
- Last location: Nakamura Tower Floor 67, 15:50 (three minutes after package delivery)
- Last message (unsent draft, found in deleted files):
  > "Chen—I fucked up. Opened the package (I know, I know, never open). It's not medical samples. It's weaponized neural pathogens. Live virus. Military bioweapon. Reeves is researching forbidden tech. I'm taking evidence to authorities. If I don't make it back, you know why. Tell my mom I—"
  (Message cuts off, never finished)
- Last call: Attempted emergency call to Chen, call interrupted/jammed after 2 seconds
- Final data burst: Location tracker forcibly disabled, 15:52

**Discovery**: Sarah discovered illegal bioweapon research, tried to expose it, was stopped before she could send evidence.

### Beat 3: The Truth
**Location**: Varies (Player pieces together what happened)
**Duration**: 3-5 minutes

**What Player Learns**:
Through investigation (combination of above tracks), player discovers:

1. **Sarah broke protocol**: Opened package during delivery (against courier code)
2. **Package contents**: Weaponized neural pathogen samples (illegal bioweapon research)
3. **Dr. Reeves**: Nakamura biotech researcher conducting forbidden military bio-research
4. **Sarah's discovery**: Realized what she was carrying, decided to expose it
5. **Corporate response**: Reeves's security detained Sarah, killed her to protect secret
6. **Body disposal**: Sarah's body likely incinerated in Nakamura medical waste (standard cleanup protocol)
7. **Cover-up**: Reeves filed delivery completion, forged Sarah's signature, erased evidence

**Player's Dilemma**: What to do with this knowledge?

### Beat 4: The Confrontation (Optional—Player Choice Determines)

**If Player Pursues Justice (Confronts Reeves or Exposes Secret)**:

**Option A: Confront Reeves Directly**

Player returns to Nakamura Tower Floor 67, confronts Dr. Reeves with evidence:

**Dr. Reeves** (drops professional mask):
> "So you figured it out. Clever. But also stupid. The courier saw something she shouldn't have. We had no choice—she was going to expose decades of research."
>
> *(Signals security via hidden button)* "And now you've made the same mistake she did. You saw too much. Security is coming. You can leave now and forget this, or you can join her in the incinerator."

**Player Options**:
- **Fight**: Combat vs. 4-6 Nakamura security (difficult, Tier 4-5 opponents)
- **Flee**: Escape Tower before lockdown (STEALTH or REFLEX checks)
- **Negotiate**: Threaten to release evidence publicly if harmed (CHARISMA + INTIMIDATION check)
- **Expose**: Upload evidence to media/resistance immediately via commlink (burns Nakamura bridges)

**Option B: Expose Research Publicly**

Player delivers evidence (Sarah's commlink data, package analysis, Reeves's research) to:
- **Independent Media**: Scandal breaks, Reeves arrested, Nakamura denies knowledge
- **Ghost Network**: Phantom distributes evidence underground, Nakamura reputation damaged
- **Authorities**: Police/regulators investigate (slow, likely get buried, but legal path)

**Consequences of Exposure**:
- +40 NAKAMURA_HOSTILITY (permanent enemy, cleanup crews hunt player)
- +30 RESISTANCE_REPUTATION (player exposed corpo crimes)
- Sarah's death acknowledged publicly (news reports: "Courier Killed Protecting Public")
- Bioweapon research shut down (or moved elsewhere, classified)
- Chen is grateful but worried: "You did right. But now you're a target too."

**If Player Buries the Truth (Walks Away)**:

**Option C: Report to Chen Only (No Public Exposure)**

Player tells Chen what happened, but doesn't expose Reeves publicly:

**Chen** (heartbroken, angry, but understands):
> "So they killed her. And they'll get away with it. Because that's how this world works."
>
> *(Wipes eyes)* "You did what you could. You found the truth. That's more than most would do."
>
> "Sarah's mom will never know what happened. I'll tell her Sarah went Rogue, started a new life somewhere. It's kinder than the truth."

**Consequences of Burying Truth**:
- No Nakamura hostility (player didn't expose them)
- Sarah's death remains secret (officially missing person, cold case)
- Bioweapon research continues (player knows, but powerless)
- Chen pays player 2,000 credits (as promised) + 2,000 bonus (appreciates finding truth)
- Moral weight: Dialogue reflects player's choice to prioritize survival over justice

**Option D: Blackmail Reeves** (Corrupt Path—Hidden)

Player contacts Reeves privately, offers deal: Silence for payment:

**Dr. Reeves**:
> "Blackmail? Refreshingly honest. How much?"
>
> [Negotiation: 10,000-20,000 credits depending on CHARISMA]
>
> "Fine. But understand—if you ever speak of this, or if copies of that evidence surface, I'll know it was you. And you'll disappear just like she did."

**Consequences of Blackmail**:
- +10,000 to 20,000 credits (blood money)
- +15 NAKAMURA_LOYALTY (player proved 'reasonable')
- No exposure (bioweapon research continues)
- Moral weight: Player sold Sarah's death for profit, heavy guilt dialogue
- Chen never learns truth (player lies: "I couldn't find anything concrete")

### Beat 5: Return to Chen
**Location**: Chen's Dispatch
**Duration**: 3-5 minutes

**Player returns to Chen with results (varies by choice):**

**If Exposed Truth**:

**Chen**:
> "I saw the news. Nakamura's denying everything, but the story's out there. Sarah's name is out there. She died trying to do the right thing."
>
> *(Grips player's shoulder)* "Thank you. For finding the truth. For making sure she mattered. And... be careful. Nakamura doesn't forget."
>
> "Here. It's not enough, but it's what I can afford." (Pays 4,000 credits—double promised amount)

**If Buried Truth**:

**Chen**:
> "So we know what happened, but the world never will. Sarah's just another missing courier. Another statistic."
>
> *(Tired)* "Maybe that's how it has to be. We survive by staying invisible. The moment we're seen, we're targets."
>
> "Thank you for looking. At least I know. That's something." (Pays 4,000 credits)

**If Blackmailed Reeves (Player Lies to Chen)**:

**Chen**:
> "Nothing? Really? You couldn't find anything?"
>
> *(Suspicious but doesn't press)* "Alright. Maybe she did go Rogue. Maybe she's alive somewhere, off-grid."
>
> "Here's your payment anyway. 2,000 for the effort." (Pays 2,000 credits)
>
> [Chen's relationship decreases if he later learns player lied—trust damaged]

**Chen's Memorial** (if truth revealed):
Chen sets up small memorial in dispatch office: Sarah's photo, candle, flowers. Other couriers leave offerings. Player can visit, reflect.

## Quest Completion

### Epilogue - Justice Path (Exposed Truth)

**Short-term**:
- Sarah's death publicly acknowledged
- Memorial service held (player can attend—emotional scene, couriers gather)
- Chen grieves but finds peace (truth honored)
- Nakamura hunt begins (random assassination attempts Tier 5-7)

**Long-term**:
- Courier community respects player (protected whistleblower, +20 COURIER_COMMUNITY_REPUTATION)
- Nakamura permanent enemy (+40 NAKAMURA_HOSTILITY)
- Bioweapon research shut down publicly (moves underground)
- Player seen as dangerous to corps (other corps avoid illegal dealings with player)

### Epilogue - Buried Truth Path

**Short-term**:
- Sarah remains missing person (no public acknowledgment)
- Chen quietly grieves (tells family she went Rogue)
- No corpo retaliation (player stayed quiet)
- Moral burden (player knows truth, world doesn't)

**Long-term**:
- Courier community never learns (Sarah forgotten except by Chen)
- Nakamura unaware of player's knowledge (neutral relationship)
- Bioweapon research continues (player sees news of mysterious illness outbreaks—implied weapon testing)
- Guilt dialogue (NPCs mention courier dangers, player thinks of Sarah)

### Epilogue - Blackmail Path

**Short-term**:
- Player gains credits (blood money)
- Chen lied to (relationship slowly degrades if truth discovered)
- Sarah's death profited from (heavy moral weight)
- Reeves continues research (player complicit)

**Long-term**:
- If Chen discovers truth: Relationship destroyed ("You sold her death for credits?")
- Nakamura has leverage over player (Reeves can threaten exposure of blackmail)
- Moral decay (player prioritized profit over justice/community)
- Bioweapon eventually used (news reports deaths, player is indirectly responsible)

## Alternative Outcomes / Failed States

### Failed Investigation (Player Can't Find Evidence)
- If player fails all investigation checks, trail goes cold
- Chen: "I know you tried. Sometimes the truth stays buried."
- Quest marked as failed (no resolution)
- Sarah remains missing (no closure)

### Player Caught During Infiltration
- If player caught snooping at Nakamura Tower, detained
- Can escape OR serve 24-hour detention
- Quest continues but harder (Nakamura suspicious)

### Combat Death (Player Loses to Security)
- If player fights Reeves's security and loses, captured
- Reeves: "You're persistent. Unfortunate. But not immortal."
- Player respawns OR escapes detention (game-dependent)
- Can retry confrontation

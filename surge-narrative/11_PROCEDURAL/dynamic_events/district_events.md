# District-Level Dynamic Events

## Purpose
15 larger-scale events that affect entire districts. These events change the world state, create new opportunities and challenges, and make the city feel dynamic and reactive. Events persist for hours or days, affecting all activities in the area.

---

## EVENT CATEGORIES

1. **Political Events** (4) - Faction actions, protests, demonstrations
2. **Security Events** (4) - Raids, lockdowns, crackdowns
3. **Infrastructure Events** (4) - Failures, disasters, construction
4. **Cultural Events** (3) - Celebrations, gatherings, ceremonies

---

## POLITICAL EVENTS

### EVENT P1: FACTION PROTEST

**Affected Districts**: Hollows, Harbor, or Uptown edges
**Duration**: 4-8 hours
**Tier Relevance**: All tiers

**Setup**:
*The streets are filling. Signs, chants, organized discontent. A faction is making a statement.*

**Protest Types**:
- **Resistance**: Anti-Algorithm, demanding integration reversal options
- **Union**: Worker rights, anti-corporate exploitation
- **Baseline**: Pro-human, against mandatory integration
- **Convergence**: Pro-Algorithm, celebrating connection

**World Effects**:
- Main thoroughfares blocked
- Security presence increased
- Certain NPCs unavailable (attending or avoiding)
- Ambient dialogue heavily themed

**Courier Impact**:
- Routes through area require detours (+15-30% delivery time)
- Some pickups/dropoffs inaccessible
- Protest-related jobs become available
- Risk of getting swept up in crowd

**Player Options**:
1. **Avoid**: Use alternate routes
2. **Push through**: Navigate crowds (time, risk)
3. **Participate**: Join protest briefly (+Faction reputation)
4. **Observe**: Document for later (information gathering)
5. **Exploit**: Work protest-related jobs (courier neutral role)

**Sample Jobs Generated**:
- Deliver supplies to protesters
- Extract someone from crowd
- Carry messages between protest leaders
- Document corporate response for journalists

### EVENT P2: FACTION SUMMIT

**Affected Districts**: Neutral zones, Old Temple area
**Duration**: 12-24 hours
**Tier Relevance**: 4+

**Setup**:
*Word spreads: faction representatives are meeting. Neutral territory. Something big is being discussed.*

**Summit Details**:
- High-security neutral zone established
- Faction leaders or representatives present
- Media blackout officially, leaks unofficially
- Tension extremely high

**World Effects**:
- Neutral zone completely locked down
- Adjacent areas heavily patrolled
- Information becomes extremely valuable
- All factions on edge

**Courier Impact**:
- No deliveries into summit zone
- Adjacent deliveries face intense scrutiny
- High-paying intelligence jobs available
- Risk of being caught between factions

**Summit-Related Jobs**:
- Deliver sealed messages to representatives
- Extract information from summit area
- Ferry observers/journalists
- Provide deniable communication channels

**Possible Outcomes**:
- Summit success: Temporary faction ceasefire
- Summit failure: Increased faction conflict
- Summit betrayal: Major story development

### EVENT P3: CORPORATE ANNOUNCEMENT

**Affected Districts**: Uptown, broadcast city-wide
**Duration**: Event day + 2-3 days aftermath
**Tier Relevance**: All tiers

**Setup**:
*Nakamura Corporation has scheduled a major announcement. Everyone's watching.*

**Announcement Types**:
- New integration program (changes Algorithm landscape)
- Policy change (affects courier operations)
- Response to crisis (damage control)
- Expansion announcement (new territory, new rules)
- Technology reveal (changes gameplay)

**World Effects**:
- All screens display announcement
- NPC conversations dominated by topic
- Stock market/economy fluctuations
- Faction responses (protests, celebrations, analysis)

**Courier Impact**:
- High demand for delivery during announcement (people distracted)
- Post-announcement rush of jobs (reactions require communication)
- Some clients unavailable (watching announcement)
- Corporate security on high alert

**Lasting Changes**:
Announcements can permanently change:
- Integration availability/cost
- Checkpoint frequency
- Job availability in certain sectors
- NPC attitudes and dialogue

### EVENT P4: UNDERGROUND MARKET DAY

**Affected Districts**: Hollows deep, rotating locations
**Duration**: 6-12 hours
**Tier Relevance**: 2+

**Setup**:
*The black market is having a big day. Goods, services, information—everything available, nothing legal.*

**Market Features**:
- Rare items for sale
- Information brokers concentrated
- Underground contacts accessible
- High-value gray market activity

**World Effects**:
- Certain tunnels/areas become market space
- Security avoids area (paid off or outmatched)
- Pickpocketing and theft increase
- Unusual goods appear in player inventory options

**Courier Impact**:
- High volume of illegal delivery work
- Excellent prices for goods
- Risk of checkpoint leaving market area
- Opportunity to make valuable contacts

**Player Opportunities**:
- Purchase rare chrome/equipment at discount
- Sell accumulated contraband at premium
- Network with underground contacts
- Accept high-paying illegal jobs
- Gather information on multiple topics

## SECURITY EVENTS

### EVENT S1: CORPORATE RAID

**Affected Districts**: Any non-Uptown
**Duration**: 2-4 hours active, 12-24 hours aftermath
**Tier Relevance**: All tiers

**Setup**:
*Corporate security is hitting somewhere. Heavy vehicles, tactical teams, the works. They're looking for something. Or someone.*

**Raid Targets**:
- Resistance safehouse
- Underground clinic
- Black market operation
- Specific individual (extraction)
- Evidence location

**World Effects**:
- Raid zone completely inaccessible
- Adjacent areas on lockdown
- Surveillance dramatically increased
- NPCs hiding, shops closing

**Courier Impact**:
- Deliveries to/from raid area impossible
- Other routes congested with people fleeing
- Some jobs become urgent (extract person/evidence before raid)
- Post-raid cleanup jobs (body disposal, evidence relocation)

**Player Involvement**:
- **Observe**: Gather intelligence safely
- **Assist raid**: Help corporate (major reputation change)
- **Oppose raid**: Help targets escape (dangerous, major reputation change)
- **Profit**: Take jobs from both sides

**Aftermath**:
- Area remains unsettled for days
- NPC casualties may include contacts
- Power dynamics shift
- New jobs emerge from wreckage

### EVENT S2: DISTRICT LOCKDOWN

**Affected Districts**: Any
**Duration**: 4-12 hours
**Tier Relevance**: All tiers

**Setup**:
*District sealed. Nobody in, nobody out. Corporate isn't saying why. Maybe nobody knows why.*

**Lockdown Reasons** (may not be revealed):
- Viral outbreak (real or suspected)
- Dangerous individual at large
- Corporate operation in progress
- Civil unrest prevention
- Infrastructure emergency

**World Effects**:
- All district borders sealed
- Checkpoints extremely aggressive
- Drone surveillance maximized
- Local services disrupted

**If Inside Lockdown**:
- Cannot leave until lifted
- Local jobs only
- Resources limited
- Tension high among trapped residents

**If Outside Lockdown**:
- Cannot access district
- Jobs to/from district impossible
- Routes around district congested
- Demand for smuggling into lockdown

**Player Options**:
- **Wait**: Lockdown eventually lifts
- **Smuggle route**: Find/use way in or out (dangerous, profitable)
- **Work locally**: Accept whatever jobs are available
- **Investigate**: Learn why lockdown is happening (information value)

### EVENT S3: ALGORITHM SWEEP

**Tier Range**: 3+ (targets integration-capable individuals)
**Affected Districts**: Any
**Duration**: 6-12 hours
**Tier Relevance**: 3-7

**Setup**:
*Algorithm enforcement teams are conducting "wellness checks." Anyone not integrated is being strongly encouraged. Or more than encouraged.*

**Sweep Details**:
- Targeting specific demographics (age, profession, district)
- "Voluntary" integration offered
- Refusal documented for "follow-up"
- Resistance hotspots raided

**World Effects**:
- Unintegrated NPCs hiding
- Integration clinics busy
- Resistance on high alert
- Baseline community terrified

**Courier Impact**:
- Unintegrated couriers face extra scrutiny
- Jobs for Resistance become critical
- Corporate jobs support sweep
- Smuggling people/information becomes priority

**If Player Unintegrated**:
- May be stopped for "wellness check"
- Options: comply with interview, flee, fake credentials
- Refusal documented (future consequences)

**If Player Integrated**:
- Passes checks easily
- May be asked to identify unintegrated contacts
- Moral weight of cooperation

### EVENT S4: POWER STRUGGLE

**Tier Range**: 4+
**Affected Districts**: Faction-controlled areas
**Duration**: 24-48 hours
**Tier Relevance**: 4-8

**Setup**:
*Leadership change within a faction. Violent or political. The transition period is dangerous for everyone.*

**Struggle Types**:
- Gang territory dispute
- Corporate executive shuffle (with purges)
- Resistance cell conflict
- Convergence schism

**World Effects**:
- Faction behavior erratic
- Allies become uncertain
- Enemies may see opportunity
- Power vacuum creates chaos

**Courier Impact**:
- Faction jobs may be honored or rejected depending on new leadership
- Some contacts disappear (dead, hiding, ascended)
- New contacts emerge
- Opportunities to curry favor with new leadership

**Player Involvement**:
- **Support old leadership**: Risky if they lose
- **Support new leadership**: Opportunistic, may pay off
- **Stay neutral**: Wait for dust to settle
- **Play both sides**: Maximum profit, maximum danger

## INFRASTRUCTURE EVENTS

### EVENT I1: POWER FAILURE

**Affected Districts**: Any
**Duration**: 2-8 hours
**Tier Relevance**: All tiers

**Setup**:
*The lights go out. The network goes down. Everything that depends on power—which is everything—stops working.*

**Affected Systems**:
- Street lighting
- Security systems
- Navigation networks
- Communication systems
- Chrome functionality

**World Effects**:
- Darkness (if nighttime)
- Increased crime
- Confusion and fear
- Emergency services overwhelmed

**Courier Impact**:
- Navigation must be done manually
- Chrome may malfunction
- Security checkpoints down (opportunity)
- Emergency delivery jobs (medical, critical)

**Player Options**:
- **Exploit darkness**: Crime or smuggling easier
- **Help community**: Emergency deliveries, assistance
- **Protect self**: Hunker down until power returns
- **Investigate**: Why did power fail? (may be sabotage)

**Chrome Complications**:
- 30% chance of chrome malfunction during blackout
- Navigation chrome particularly affected
- Combat chrome may have targeting issues

---

### EVENT I2: NETWORK CRASH

**Affected Districts**: Variable (network topology)
**Duration**: 4-12 hours
**Tier Relevance**: All tiers

**Setup**:
*The network is down. Not just slow—down. No job board, no navigation, no communication. Old school only.*

**Crash Types**:
- Sector-specific (one area)
- Service-specific (one function)
- Complete blackout (everything)
- Corrupted (network active but unreliable)

**World Effects**:
- Job assignments must be in-person
- Communication by voice/physical message only
- Many NPCs unable to function
- Dependence on Algorithm revealed starkly

**Courier Impact**:
- Must get jobs directly from Chen or contacts
- Delivery confirmation manual
- Navigation by memory/landmarks
- Competitors also affected

**Opportunity**:
- Pre-network skills become valuable
- Information becomes extremely valuable
- Physical message delivery premium
- Those who can function without network excel

### EVENT I3: CONSTRUCTION ZONE

**Affected Districts**: Any
**Duration**: Days to weeks
**Tier Relevance**: All tiers

**Setup**:
*Major construction project. A route you've used forever is now a maze of barriers, equipment, and workers.*

**Construction Types**:
- Corporate expansion (new building)
- Infrastructure repair (road, tunnel)
- Security installation (checkpoints, walls)
- Demolition (clearing space)

**World Effects**:
- Route changes (permanent or temporary)
- Noise and chaos in area
- Worker NPCs present
- Security around construction

**Courier Impact**:
- Major route adjustments required
- Delivery times to area increased
- Some addresses temporarily inaccessible
- Construction workers as potential contacts

**Evolution**:
- Construction progresses over game time
- New routes may open
- Area character may change permanently
- Early disruption leads to later convenience (or new problems)

---

### EVENT I4: ENVIRONMENTAL DISASTER

**Affected Districts**: Harbor, industrial areas, or any
**Duration**: Hours to days
**Tier Relevance**: All tiers

**Setup**:
*Something went very wrong. Chemical spill, industrial accident, or worse. The area is dangerous.*

**Disaster Types**:
- Chemical leak (toxic zone)
- Explosion (structural damage, fire)
- Biological hazard (quarantine)
- Radiation leak (chrome interference)
- Flooding (coastal areas)

**World Effects**:
- Area evacuation (some comply, some don't)
- Emergency services overwhelmed
- Resource scarcity in affected area
- Long-term health concerns

**Courier Impact**:
- Hazard zone deliveries extremely dangerous
- Evacuation assistance jobs
- Medical supply runs critical
- Long-term area may be abandoned/changed

**Player Options**:
- **Avoid**: Safest, miss opportunities
- **Hazard pay runs**: High risk, high reward
- **Rescue work**: Help evacuate (karma, contacts)
- **Exploitation**: Looting, scavenging (profit, karma cost)

## CULTURAL EVENTS

### EVENT C1: INTEGRATION CEREMONY

**Affected Districts**: Convergence areas, public spaces
**Duration**: 4-8 hours
**Tier Relevance**: All tiers (3+ for participation)

**Setup**:
*The Convergence is holding a public integration ceremony. New members joining the Algorithm. Celebration for some, mourning for others.*

**Ceremony Features**:
- Public integration of willing participants
- Convergence speeches and music
- Counter-protesters at edges
- Heavy media presence

**World Effects**:
- Convergence areas festive
- Resistance areas somber
- Public opinion visibly divided
- Algorithm presence intensified

**Courier Impact**:
- Ceremony area congested
- Convergence jobs available (support ceremony)
- Resistance jobs available (counter-actions)
- Neutral delivery work affected by crowds

**Emotional Weight**:
- May witness familiar NPCs integrating
- See families divided by choice
- Propaganda from multiple sides
- Player's own status highlighted

### EVENT C2: MEMORIAL DAY

**Affected Districts**: Hollows, Harbor, cemetery areas
**Duration**: Full day
**Tier Relevance**: All tiers

**Setup**:
*A day of remembrance. For those lost to integration failures, faction violence, or just the grind of city life.*

**Memorial Focus** (varies):
- Integration failure victims
- Courier memorial (fallen runners)
- Faction casualties
- General city dead

**World Effects**:
- Somber atmosphere
- Many NPCs unavailable (attending memorials)
- Violence reduced (informal ceasefire)
- Stories shared, history revealed

**Courier Impact**:
- Fewer jobs available
- Memorial-related deliveries (flowers, items)
- Access to normally unavailable NPCs
- Reflection on player's own journey

- Learn about city history
- Meet older NPCs with perspective
- Discover connections between events

### EVENT C3: STREET FESTIVAL

**Affected Districts**: Any (rotating)
**Duration**: 6-12 hours
**Tier Relevance**: All tiers

**Setup**:
*Life isn't all darkness. The district is celebrating—music, food, dancing, temporary joy.*

**Festival Types**:
- Cultural celebration (ethnic community)
- Harvest festival (surviving agricultural tradition)
- Founding day (district anniversary)
- Impromptu celebration (good news, relief)

**World Effects**:
- Streets transformed by celebration
- NPCs relaxed, available for conversation
- Security lighter (or focused on crowd)
- Unusual vendors and performers

**Courier Impact**:
- Festival area congested but friendly
- Event-related delivery jobs (supplies, messages)
- Tips more generous
- Network expanded (people more open)

**Player Options**:
- **Work through**: Deliveries continue, crowds slow you
- **Participate**: Take time off, build relationships
- **Exploit crowds**: Pickpocket opportunities (karma cost)
- **Use distraction**: Security gaps for other activities

## IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

### Event Scheduling
- 1-2 district events active at any time
- Events can overlap in different districts
- Some events chain (raid → power struggle)
- Major story events trigger specific district events

### World State Integration
District events should affect:
- Job availability
- Route efficiency
- NPC availability
- Dialogue options
- Shop inventory

### Player Agency
- Players cannot cause most events
- Players can influence outcomes
- Player actions during events have lasting consequences
- Ignoring events is valid but may have costs

### Notification System
- Events announced through:
  - Chen's board (major events)
  - NPC conversation (medium events)
  - Environmental observation (minor events)
  - Network alerts (if connected)

### Persistence
- Event outcomes saved to world state
- Some events create permanent changes
- NPC memories of player actions during events
- Reputation effects from event involvement

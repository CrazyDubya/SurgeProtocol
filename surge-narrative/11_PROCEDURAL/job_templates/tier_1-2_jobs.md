# Tier 1-2 Job Templates

## Purpose
15 procedural job templates for early career couriers. Steps up complexity from Tier 0 while remaining manageable.

---

## JOB TEMPLATE 1: CLINIC RESUPPLY

### Overview
**Tier**: 1
**Type**: Medical
**Urgency**: Standard
**Pay**: 65 credits

### Briefing
**Chen's Board**:
```
JOB: Clinic Resupply Run
PICKUP: Harbor Pharmacy
DROPOFF: Hollows Free Clinic
PACKAGE: Medical supplies (standard kit)
DEADLINE: 45 minutes
PAY: 65 credits
```

**Chen** (optional comment): "Clinic's always running low. Regular work. Good for building rep."

### Pickup Flavor
**Pharmacist**: "Another clinic run? They go through supplies fast. Here—standard kit. Already packed."

### Route Notes
- Harbor to Hollows, standard route
- Possible checkpoint delay
- Weather may be factor

### Dropoff Flavor
**Clinic Worker**: "More supplies! Perfect. Dr. Yusuf will be relieved. Thanks, courier."

### Complications (10% chance)
- Checkpoint inspection (brief delay)
- Clinic location changed temporarily (redirect message)

## TEMPLATE USAGE NOTES

### Selection Frequency
- Templates 1-5, 9-10, 13, 15: Common (Tier 1 standard)
- Templates 6-8, 11-12, 14: Less common (Tier 2 introduction)

### Combination with Core Systems
- Client profiles from `client_profiles.md` can modify dialogue
- Package types from `package_descriptions.md` can add variety
- Circumstances from `delivery_circumstances.md` add complications

### Scaling
- Pay can vary ±15% based on relationship with source
- Deadlines can tighten/loosen based on urgency
- Complications probability adjustable by difficulty setting

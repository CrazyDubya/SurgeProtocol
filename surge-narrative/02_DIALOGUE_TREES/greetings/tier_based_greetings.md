# Tier-Based Greetings

## Overview

Tier (0-10) represents player progression and reputation. As players climb tiers, NPCs treat them with increasing respect, fear, or caution. These greetings reflect the player's growing legend in the courier world.

---

## Tier Progression

- **Tier 0**: Rookie, untested, expendable
- **Tier 1-2**: Proven survivor, earning respect
- **Tier 3-4**: Established professional, notable reputation
- **Tier 5-6**: Elite courier, significant augmentation
- **Tier 7-8**: Legendary status, inhuman capabilities
- **Tier 9**: Approaching apotheosis, feared and revered
- **Tier 10**: Transcendent, no longer fully human

## Implementation Notes

**Tier Transition Events:**

When player gains a tier, trigger special conversations:

**Tier 1:** Chen congratulates, warns about complacency
**Tier 3:** Tanaka's cochlear implant warning
**Tier 5:** Lopez's "don't sell out" speech
**Tier 7:** Okonkwo appears, Interstitial introduction
**Tier 9:** Solomon appears, Third Path introduction
**Tier 10:** Final choice consequences play out

**Dynamic Tier Recognition:**

NPCs should acknowledge recent tier advancement:
"Heard you just hit Tier [X]. Congratulations. How's it feel?"

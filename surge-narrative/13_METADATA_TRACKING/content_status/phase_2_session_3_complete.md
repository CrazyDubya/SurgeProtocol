## Quest Design Principles (Maintained)

### Simpler Than Milestones
- ✅ 2-3 branching paths (vs. 4-5 in milestones)
- ✅ Linear core with choice moments (vs. complex branching)
- ✅ Character focus over system complexity
- ✅ Thematic consistency with overall arc

### Connector Function
- ✅ Bridge major milestones (0→3, 3→6, etc.)
- ✅ Develop relationships (Chen, Rosa progression)
- ✅ Introduce recurring characters (Jin, Delilah, Okonkwo)
- ✅ Foreshadow future content (Solomon, Ghost Network, Factions)

### Thematic Depth
Each quest explores core question: **"What are you willing to trade?"**
- **Tier 1**: Trade mercy for success? (Help Jin or win)
- **Tier 2**: Trade ethics for profit? (Gray-market work)
- **Tier 4**: Trade autonomy for efficiency? (Trust Algorithm)
- **Tier 5**: Trade connection for isolation? (Stay in system or discover alternatives)
